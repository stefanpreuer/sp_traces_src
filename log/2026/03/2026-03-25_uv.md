Finally a proper package manager for Python? At least *[uv][1]* looks very promising.

It is inspired by Rust's excelent Cargo package and project manager. Also it is implemented in Rust, indicating that it is fast and of quality.

Due to its excellent documentation[^1] there is not much to mention here about how to use it and the concepts it is based on.
