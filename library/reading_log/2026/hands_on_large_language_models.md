---
title: Hands-on Large Language Models
---

Hands-on Large Language Models[^AlammarGrootendorst2024] is an eye-opener book for me in terms of overview and how LLMs enable all kinds of applications. The most important insight I gained from this book is how conceptually trivial more advanced results can be achieved, even by just (dynamically) chaining together well crafted promts. Even simple agentic can be achieved by this, assuming some basic tool integration.

I knew the inner workings of GPT models quite good by books like Raschka (2024)[^Raschka2024].

[^AlammarGrootendorst2024]: Jay Alammar, Marteen Grootendorst (2024). Hands-On Large Language Models
[^Raschka2024]: Sebastian Raschka (2024). Build a Large Language Model From Scratch
